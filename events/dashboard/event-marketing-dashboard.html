Highest Pipeline: CTC Summit ($236K ARR)

Watch: DTC Live London (310 contacts, 0 deals)
Generated 53% of all contacts but zero deals. At $17/contact it was the cheapest lead source, but conversion is non-existent. Investigate whether this is a lead quality issue, attribution gap, or whether follow-up processes need improvement.
Watch: League of Originals ($33,304 spend, $0 closed-won)
Largest single-event spend with no closed revenue yet. However, $57,864 ARR is in active pipeline across 3 deals (Nomad, Bala, GEM). ROI depends entirely on closing these. Prioritize these 3 deals.
Underperformers: Shoptalk (0.44x ROI)
$28,225 spent but only $12,444 closed-won ARR (2 small wins). However, $42,000 in open Dr Squatch pipeline could change this. If both Dr Squatch deals close, ROI improves to 1.93x.
San Diego Small Ecom ($750 spend, $19,584 pipeline ARR)

Zero-Return Event: Klaviyo Boston ($5,232 spend)
10 contacts generated, but zero deals and zero pipeline. Reconsider similar events without a clear deal path, or investigate whether follow-up was adequate.
