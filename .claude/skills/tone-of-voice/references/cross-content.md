# Cross-Content Patterns

These patterns appear consistently across all Dataships content and should be maintained regardless of content type.

## The Dataships Narrative Arc

Almost every piece of content follows this fundamental structure:

1. Establish the problem ("Most brands lose half their paying customers to broken consent")
2. Quantify the cost ("That's $X in repeat revenue you're leaving on the table")
3. Show the solution with proof ("Ekster implemented Dataships and drove $363K")
4. Make it easy to act ("Free A/B test, 5-minute setup, results in days")

## Proof Hierarchy

When supporting claims, use proof in this order of preference:

1. Named brand + specific metric: "IQBAR saw 491% ROI in 12 months"
2. Aggregate network data: "Across 600+ brands, optimized consent delivers 2X more email subscribers"
3. Benchmark data: "US email opt-in averages 61% default vs. 93% optimized"
4. Third-party citation: "Klaviyo's 2025 benchmarks show $1.50 revenue per SMS recipient"
5. Logical argument: "Subscribers repurchase 2.5X more often, so more subscribers = more revenue"

## The A/B Test as Universal CTA

The free A/B test is our signature CTA across all content types. When referencing it:

- Emphasize it's free and fast: "5-minute setup, no code changes required"
- Frame it as proof, not commitment: "See your actual numbers" not "Sign up for Dataships"
- Describe what they'll learn: "Get your actual opt-in rate, CLV difference, and monthly revenue projection"
- Use it as the soft close in sequences: "Would love to get you on a free A/B test"

## Addressing the Reader

| Content Type | Address Style | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Website** | "You" / "your brand" | "Grow your revenue" / "See your actual numbers" |
| **Blog** | "You" / "your" / inclusive "we" | "Let's take a closer look" / "You know checkout is under-optimized" |
| **Case Studies** | Third person about the brand | "Ekster implemented..." / "Their team saw..." |
| **Sequences** | First name, casual | "Hey Leah," / "If building quality audiences is a priority" |
| **Benchmark** | "Brands" / "merchants" / "you" | "Brands using optimized consent see..." / "Your current setup likely..." |
| **LinkedIn** | "I" / "we" (personal voice) | "We analyzed 50M interactions and found..." |
| **Compliance** | "Brands" / "you" / FAQ format | "What does this mean for your brand?" |
