## Best-Fit Signals
- Selling globally (US + EU/UK) — compliance complexity drives urgency
- High repeat purchase model (drops, subscriptions, consumables)
- Large existing email/SMS list — more to lose from noncompliance
- Currently using Shopify native consent — biggest uplift opportunity
- Recently expanded internationally — compliance is new and scary
- Director/VP-level evaluator — infrastructure buyer, not feature buyer

## Disqualifying Signals
- Not on Shopify Plus
- Extremely low order volume (< 1,000/month)
- No email/SMS marketing program
- Single-purchase products with no repeat dynamics
